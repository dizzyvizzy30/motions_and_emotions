# Website Design Proposal for Ashay Parikh

This proposal outlines a new design for the Motions and Emotions website, customized for Ashay Parikh.

## Color Scheme

*   **Primary Color:** Deep Blue (`#2c3e50`)
*   **Secondary Color:** Light Gray (`#ecf0f1`)
*   **Accent Color:** Vibrant Turquoise (`#1abc9c`)
*   **Text Color:** Dark Gray (`#34495e`)

## Typography

*   **Headings:** Montserrat
*   **Body:** Open Sans

## Layout

### Header

A clean and simple header with the name "Ashay Parikh" on the left and navigation links on the right. The "Portfolio" link will have a dropdown menu containing categories (Air Show, Baby Shower, Engagement, Nature, Sky Scrapers).

### Hero Section

A full-width hero image with the title "Motions & Emotions" and a short tagline "Fine Art Photography by Ashay Parikh".

### Portfolio Section

A grid of images. The filtering of images by category will be controlled by the dropdown menu in the header. When an image is clicked, it will open in a lightbox.

### About Section

A section with a picture of Ashay Parikh on the left and a short bio on the right.

### Contact Section

A simple contact form with fields for name, email, and message. A map showing the location of Ashay Parikh will be on the right.

### Footer

A simple footer with social media links and copyright information.
