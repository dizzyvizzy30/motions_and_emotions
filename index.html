Capturing Life As It Happens

By Ashay Parikh

MY PHILOSOPHY

WE LIVE LIFE
THAT IS WORTH CAPTURING

Greetings, I capture life as it happens. The gentle sway of a dress in the breeze, the blur of a cityscape at dusk, the unbridled laughter that moves through a family—these are the motions that tell our story. But just as important are the emotions that fill those moments: the quiet joy in a lover's gaze, the heartfelt tear during a ceremony, the pure excitement of a child at play. My passion is to capture both. I see photography not as a way to freeze time, but as a way to preserve the energy and feeling of life as it unfolds. It’s about bottling the beautiful chaos of movement and the profound stillness of emotion, creating images that are not just seen, but felt.
